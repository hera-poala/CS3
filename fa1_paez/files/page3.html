Badminton

  • a game with rackets in which a shuttlecock is played back and forth across a net
  • A point is scored when the shuttlecock lands inside the opponent's court or if a returned shuttlecock hits the net or lands outside of the court the player will lose the point.
  • I used to play this with my classmates after class.
  • One struggling part of the game is when the shuttlecock gets stuck somewhere tall.
